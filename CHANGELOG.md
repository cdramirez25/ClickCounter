## [v1.0.0] - 2025-03-16

### Added
- Click counter label (`CC: X`) displayed in the top-left corner during gameplay
- Counter resets automatically on every retry
- Counter clears when exiting a level
- Counts all inputs: jump, secondary button, dual mode
- Only counts presses (not releases) to avoid double counting
